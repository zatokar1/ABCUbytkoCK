Green Room

Double room with option for two extra beds or a child's cot. Southwest-facing windows with balcony.

About this room

The Green Room is a spacious double room with a southwest-facing balcony. Its position provides beautiful views and plenty of daylight throughout the day.

The room offers the option of two extra beds or a child's cot, making it ideal for families with children. An en-suite bathroom with shower and WC is included.
