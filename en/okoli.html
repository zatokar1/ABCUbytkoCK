Surroundings & Attractions

Český Krumlov and its surroundings offer countless experiences for every visitor.

Heritage

Castles & Chateaux

Discover the rich history of South Bohemia through its impressive castles and chateaux.

Český Krumlov Castle

A monumental castle and chateau complex perched on a rocky hill above the Vltava river. One of the largest castle complexes in Central Europe, listed as a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Rožmberk Castle

A 13th-century castle featuring the Gothic Jakobínka tower. Today it houses a weapons collection and family gallery. It was inhabited until 1945.

Dívčí Kámen Castle Ruins

A 14th-century Gothic castle once inhabited by the Rožmberk family. Now accessible ruins divided into upper and lower sections, a popular excursion destination.

Vítkův Hrádek Castle Ruins

A border fortress built around 1250, maintained until the early 18th century. A popular tourist destination in the region with beautiful views.

Activities

Things to Do

From water sports to skiing — there's never a dull moment around Český Krumlov.

🎭

Revolving Theatre

A unique revolving auditorium in the castle gardens. An unforgettable cultural experience under the open sky.

🎶

Festivals

The Five-Petalled Rose Festival and other cultural events that bring the historic town centre to life.

🛶

Water Sports

Rafting and canoeing on the Vltava river. Lipno lake offers swimming, windsurfing and other water activities.

⛷️

Skiing

In winter, take advantage of ski resorts near Lipno and the Šumava mountains.

🚴

Cycling

Numerous cycle paths through the beautiful South Bohemian landscape, including a trail along the Vltava river.

🏞️

Nature

Šumava and the surrounding forests offer countless hiking trails for all fitness levels.
