About Us

Welcome to Guesthouse ABC

We offer comfortable private accommodation in Český Krumlov. The guesthouse is located in a quiet part of town, approximately 15 minutes on foot from the historic centre.

We have three rooms and one apartment with a total capacity of up to 13 guests. A shared dining area with a refrigerator is available for a comfortable stay.

Your hosts: Milada and František Kadlečkovi
