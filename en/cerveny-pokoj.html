Red Room

Double room with option for one extra bed or a child's cot. East-facing windows.

About this room

The Red Room is a cosy double room with the option of one extra bed or a child's cot. The windows face east, so you can enjoy the morning sunshine.

The room has its own en-suite bathroom (WC and shower), television and wireless internet. Ideal for couples or small families looking for peaceful accommodation near the centre of Český Krumlov.
